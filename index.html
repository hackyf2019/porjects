Hakan's Recipe Book

There are lots of different food recipes in this page. I like to travel a lot around the World and discover different food types of each culture. Some food types are very spicy and some are very strange even to taste a bit. Some food types might be accepted as bad and disgusting while some people find them very delicious. It is very common to eat insects in far east while it is totaly awkward in some cultures.


Eating meat is very common in most of arabian countries while vegetable is more preferred in most european cultures. Cat, dog, or snake are eatable in some countries.

There are some countries that fruits are part of daily eating more than meat and vegetables. Because fruit industry is developed very well and the prices of all fruit types is quite cheap. Some fruits might also contain more benefits for human health.

Etli Kuru Fasulye

This is a traditional Turkish food. It is NOT a vegan food. There is meat used as ingredient.

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 5 person
Ingredients - Quantity - Cooking Time
Beans 1kg 1.5 hours
Onion 2 pieces 5 mins
Meat 500 gr 20 mins
Spice-Salt Tea spoon At the end

How to Cook: Keep dry beans for a night in warm water. This will reduce cooking time of beans and softened them. Add some vegetable oil, and sliced meat. Cook the meat until it becomes soft enough to eat easily. After that add sliced onion and tomatoes paste into cooking pot and cook them all together for around 10 minutes. Add softened beans after filtering from water and cook together for another 5 minutes with meat. Finally add enough water and salt and continue cooking for another hour until all beans becomes completely softened.
